our agency

who are we

AGOS is a company specializing in security and protection services, risk management in order to ensure the safety of goods and people. Our conception of security services is distinguished by our mastery of risk factors and the management of a set of parameters capable of creating an ideal secure environment. For AGOS, mastering crisis situations is a strategic asset. We have thus developed an innovative human surveillance concept.

our mission

Our motto is simply; a unique solution for a unique client. Our security solutions, therefore, are based on the industry needs and expectations of our customers. Systemic risk analysis and personalized auditing guarantee innovative, effective and unique solutions for corporations as well as individuals.

For AGOS, it is about providing “tailor-made” security solutions. AGOS sets itself apart and invites its customers to reflect on their surveillance and security policies.

AGOS indeed encourages the initiatives of its security agents and its management to constantly improve us. We take a serious commitment towards our employees, because they make our success. We seek to create and maintain an environment where each employee feels involved and empowered. AGOS provides a wide range of advancement opportunities as recognition and rewards to every dedicated employee in order to further his career.

industrial and manufacturing plants

New regulations require manufacturers to provide an adequate and safe work environment for employees. The presence of a security guard has become an absolute requirement to ensure surveillance and prevent risks.

AGOS provides companies with security guards who are able to perform the following tasks

  • Control access of customers.
  • Carry out security checks.
  • Ensure public safety and security.
  • Identify security threats.
  • Manage major incidents.
  • Be vigilant.
  • Work in teams.
  • Controlling official documents.
  • Manage incident reporting files.
  • Conducting searches for staff.

In addition to these activities, our agents provide services in the import/export area. They collaborate with the import/export manager and assist him in the management of his daily missions and tasks.

AGOS provides factories and industries with mobile patrol agents who mainly carry out the following activities

  • Carry out surveillance rounds to prevent malicious acts and detect risks of fire or intrusion.
  • Intervene in case of doubt and point out the origin of the alarm.
  • Notify or arrange for the notification of the departments or persons concerned in the event of a major incident.
  • Carry out the appropriate guard actions and ensure the continuity of the protection of the site according to the predetermined instructions and/or the instructions of the remote monitoring center.

All of our agents have undergone advanced training to be able to perform the following tasks

  • Drafting of safety instructions and reports.
  • Assessment of potential and future risks.
  • Mastery of the knowledge related to risk prevention.
  • Full knowledge of safety regulations, standards, and legislation.
  • Analysis of accidents and incidents for all types of activities.
  • Definition of prevention and intervention tools against any type of theft, leakage, or fraud within the company.
  • Ability to communicate and assert oneself.

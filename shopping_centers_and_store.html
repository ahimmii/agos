shopping centers and store

Shopping malls by definition are very busy and attract a large number of customers. They are there to enjoy their free time and are not concerned about possible risks or dangers. It is up to the shopping centers to act and ensure their safety.

AGOS offers effective security solutions to protect thousands of square meters of retail space and make the shopping experience a new one.

We share one motto: create a safe environment for your customers and staff and reduce theft, loitering, and vandalism. Our security guards are carefully selected and trained. They are vigilant and efficient in protecting your businesses and assisting your customers. However, to meet the needs of a particularly demanding sector of activity, AGOS guarantees permanent availability 7 days a week and 24 hours a day and the implementation of adapted solutions:

- A selective recruitment and training of the agents on a theoretical and practical basis

- A regular follow-up of the service with reports and corrective actions maintained.

- An action plan defined in advance and systematically updated to meet changing needs and to assist our agents in the execution of their tasks.

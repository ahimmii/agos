MOBILE PATROL

For AGOS, the mobile patrol is a key element in the success of the complete security system, it ensures a better protection of your facilities and assets. AGOS mobile patrols are always at the right place and time.

Our patrollers perform surveillance and inspection tasks according to a predetermined or random schedule. They are immediately on site when needed and when an alarm condition is triggered.

Our patrol supervisors schedule visits at fixed or arbitrary times to ensure that your sites are safe and secure, and they come in marked and well-maintained security vehicles. AGOS places special emphasis on the training and qualification of its patrol supervisors to ensure total security. You can, therefore, count on our mobile patrol units to secure all your sites.

AGOS provides real-time security reports on every incident via e-mail through our time-stamped patrol reporting and verification system, so you can be assured of our total commitment.

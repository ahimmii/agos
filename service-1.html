executive protection in morocco

To ensure the safety of personalities exposed to a high level of risk, the executive protection service is essential. This means the effective protection of people via guardrails or the safe transport of people.

At AGOS, executive protection is a necessary measure for people who may be exposed to a potential risk to their job, reputation, wealth, social status, or any other reason that could be subject to a physical attack or various dangers.

We have, therefore, carefully trained guardrail teams. For AGOS, each client is treated in a personalized and unique way, considering his environment, the nature of the risk to which he is exposed, and the security information provided beforehand. In addition to physical protection services, our executive and VIP protection solutions involve detailed planning and advance preparation using new counter-surveillance and analysis methods.

Our transport teams are composed of qualified and experienced professionals with perfect knowledge of the areas and routes. We propose a thorough study of the visited area and an analysis of the itineraries during each trip. In the event of an incident, we ensure the immediate change of vehicle even during the journey. In addition, we provide a diversified transport fleet to match each client's different profiles and specific needs. Our expertise is reflected in the confidence of many potential clients nationally and internationally.

Our executive protection service is available for the main cities of Morocco: Marrakech, Casablanca, Rabat, and Tangier. For more information, please contact us.

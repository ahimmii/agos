events and venues

The event world regularly mobilizes large crowds. Security is then a priority that pushes the organizers to take into account all the potential risks and to set up adequate devices to maintain the safety of the public and the participants.

To secure your events and demonstrations, AGOS provides you with security solutions adapted to the nature of the event, from an upstream organization with the recommendation of security instructions to the complete security of the event.

AGOS offers complete and adapted services, to protect you from non-deliberate incidents and malicious acts. Our goal is to make your event a success.

Our event security officers go through a rigorous screening process and undergo advanced training to be able to perform the following tasks:

  • Provide information on security measures.
  • Securing the outside area.
  • Secure the queues.
  • Secure access and interior areas.
  • Securing exits and parking areas.
  • Provide executive protection for VIPs.

We also hire all the necessary and adapted equipment for optimal security of your event. Our agents have the specific equipment and tools to accomplish the missions that are entrusted to them.

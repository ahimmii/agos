SECURE EXECUTIVE GROUND TRANSPORTATION

Nowadays finding transportation is all about one click of a button. However, finding reliable and world-class secure transportation services can be a real hustle.

AGOS specializes in secure ground transportation & executive protection. We fulfill the need for first-class traveling and elite traveling, which will reflect the appropriate image of your entity. Whether for business or personal traveling, our trained and vetted drivers are at your service to meet your expectations securely and professionally.

WHY AGOS SECURE GROUND
TRANSPORTATION SERVICES

Providing an elite service means ensuring the best drivers are behind the wheel on every trip. To make it happen, all of our drivers must undergo a strict vetting process, which includes the following:

in-person interview

driving record review

background check

drug testing

social media audit

Once the driver passes the initial selection process; they are invited to complete AGOS driving & safety training program. The training program consists of customer service and communication techniques, confidentiality, personal presentation, vehicle presentation, client care, driving protocol, and emergency procedures.

At AGOS we try to be your preferred counselor when it comes to secure executive ground transportation. As such, we are here to offer flexibility, leverage our expertise to optimize your programs, and take the responsibility of managing ground needs off your shoulders.

Our capabilities extend far beyond basic travel needs. If you have an exclusive request, feel free to tell us in detail. We will be happy to chat about how we might assist.

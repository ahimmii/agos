residential highrise buildings and compounds

Modern residential complexes are a tasty target for intruders. The presence of new facilities, parking lots, elevator equipment, and other costly infrastructure can become the object of encroachment. To make your complex safe and comfortable, you can't do without professionals. AGOS is committed to providing the best security service thanks to its know-how in the field of security management of residential complexes.

AGOS takes into consideration the peculiarity of security and protection within a residential complex since the contract is usually concluded with a legal entity (condominium corporation), but in fact, each owner of the apartment is also a full participant in the transaction and has the right and opportunity to influence the process of organizing security measures and his expectations must be met.

The main task is to ensure maximum security for owners, tenants, and their property, as well as the creation of a friendly and supportive environment so that people feel comfortable. In order to achieve this, the security guards of our company AGOS rigorously watch over :

- Monitoring the entrances and exits of the main doors.

- Checking the general condition of the buildings and the functioning of their equipment.

- Alert and intervene in case of fire.

- Collecting residents' complaints.

- Writing reports in case of incidents or general dysfunction.
